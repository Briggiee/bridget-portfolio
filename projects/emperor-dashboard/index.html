Emperor Logistics & Supply Chain Performance Dashboard

A comprehensive logistics and supply chain analytics dashboard developed using Power BI to monitor orders, deliveries, procurement, inventory, fleet performance, financial operations, customer service and returns.

Project Type

Business Intelligence Dashboard

Industry

Logistics & Supply Chain

Tools

Power BI, DAX, Power Query, Excel

Role

Data Analyst & BI Developer

Project Overview

This Business Intelligence solution was designed to provide management with a centralized view of logistics and supply chain operations. The dashboard combines order fulfillment, procurement performance, inventory visibility, fleet monitoring, financial cost tracking, and customer service metrics into a single interactive reporting solution that supports operational and strategic decision-making.

Key Business Metrics

The dashboard provides decision-makers with a comprehensive view of logistics and supply chain performance by tracking order fulfillment, procurement spending, stock levels, fleet activity, operational costs, customer returns and service quality.

📦 Orders & Deliveries

Tracks total orders, deliveries and fulfillment performance to evaluate logistics execution.

🧾 Procurement Cost

Monitors purchase spending, supplier activity and procurement trends across the business.

🏬 Inventory Levels

Provides visibility into stock quantity, warehouse distribution and inventory availability.

🚛 Fleet Operations

Evaluates vehicle availability, shipment activity, transport performance and delivery support capacity.

💰 Financial Performance

Measures purchase cost, fuel cost, maintenance cost and total logistics operating cost.

📞 Customer Service & Returns

Monitors return volume, return cost, return reasons and customer satisfaction trends.

📈 Operational Trends

Analyzes trends across orders, purchases, inventory and fleet activity to support planning and optimization.

📊 Executive KPIs

Provides a consolidated view of the most important supply chain and logistics performance indicators.

Technologies Used

Power BI DAX Power Query Excel

Dashboard Capabilities

  • 📦 Orders & Deliveries Monitoring
  • 🧾 Procurement & Supplier Analysis
  • 🏬 Inventory & Warehousing Visibility
  • 🚛 Fleet & Transport Performance Tracking
  • 💰 Financial Cost Monitoring
  • 📞 Customer Service & Returns Analysis
  • 📊 Executive KPI Reporting
  • 📈 Interactive Filtering & Trend Analysis

Business Impact

This dashboard enables logistics and supply chain decision-makers to monitor operational performance through a centralized reporting solution. By consolidating orders, deliveries, procurement, inventory, fleet, financial and customer service metrics into one interactive dashboard, management can identify cost drivers, monitor service quality, improve operational efficiency and make faster data-driven decisions.
