Manufacturing Intelligence Dashboard

A comprehensive executive dashboard developed using Power BI to monitor production, sales, inventory, workforce performance and supply chain operations.

Project Type

Business Intelligence Dashboard

Industry

Manufacturing

Tools

Power BI, DAX, Power Query, Excel

Role

Data Analyst & BI Developer

Project Overview

This Business Intelligence solution was designed to provide executives with a centralized view of manufacturing operations. The dashboard combines production performance, inventory monitoring, sales analysis, workforce productivity and supply chain metrics into a single interactive reporting solution.

Key Business Metrics

The dashboard provides executives with a comprehensive view of manufacturing performance by tracking production efficiency, inventory, workforce productivity, sales performance, and supply chain operations.

🏭 Production Output

Monitors total production volume to evaluate manufacturing performance.

📦 Inventory Levels

Tracks available inventory to maintain optimal stock levels and reduce shortages.

💰 Sales Performance

Measures revenue generation and overall sales performance across products.

🚚 Supply Chain Efficiency

Evaluates supplier performance, logistics efficiency, and delivery operations.

👨‍💼 Workforce Productivity

Assesses employee productivity and operational workforce performance.

📈 Production Trends

Analyzes production trends over time to support forecasting and planning.

⚙️ Operational Efficiency

Measures operational performance through key manufacturing efficiency indicators.

📊 Executive KPIs

Provides a consolidated view of the most important business performance indicators.

Technologies Used

Power BI DAX Power Query Excel

Dashboard Capabilities

  • 📊 Executive KPI Monitoring
  • 🏭 Production Performance Tracking
  • 📦 Inventory Analysis
  • 💰 Sales Performance Analysis
  • 🚚 Supply Chain Monitoring
  • 👨‍💼 Workforce Productivity
  • 📈 Interactive Filtering & Drill-down
  • 📉 Trend Analysis & Forecast Support

Business Impact

This dashboard enables manufacturing executives to monitor operational performance through a centralized reporting solution. By consolidating production, sales, inventory, workforce and supply chain metrics into one interactive dashboard, decision-makers can identify trends, monitor KPIs, improve operational efficiency and make faster data-driven decisions.
