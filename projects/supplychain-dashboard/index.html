Supply Chain Performance Dashboard

A business intelligence dashboard that provides visibility into supplier performance, logistics operations, delivery efficiency, and procurement activities to improve supply chain decision-making.

Project Overview

This Supply Chain Performance Dashboard was developed to provide organizations with real-time visibility into procurement activities, supplier performance, logistics operations, and delivery efficiency. The dashboard transforms supply chain data into meaningful insights, enabling decision-makers to monitor supplier reliability, optimize logistics performance, improve delivery timelines, and support data-driven operational planning.

Key Business Metrics

The dashboard tracks essential supply chain metrics that provide visibility into procurement, supplier performance, logistics operations, and delivery efficiency.

🚚 Delivery Performance

Measures on-time deliveries and logistics efficiency.

🤝 Supplier Performance

Evaluates supplier reliability and procurement effectiveness.

📦 Shipment Volume

Tracks total shipments processed across the supply chain.

⏱ Lead Time

Measures the time taken from procurement to delivery.

🌍 Logistics Performance

Monitors transportation efficiency and operational performance.

📋 Procurement Activity

Tracks purchasing activities and supplier transactions.

📈 Delivery Trends

Analyzes delivery performance over time.

⚙️ Supply Chain Efficiency

Provides an overall view of operational supply chain performance.

Business Impact

This dashboard provides supply chain managers and business leaders with a centralized view of procurement, logistics, supplier performance, and delivery operations. By delivering real-time visibility into transportation efficiency, supplier reliability, shipment performance, and procurement activities, the solution supports faster decision-making, improves operational efficiency, and strengthens overall supply chain performance.
