Inventory Analytics Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard developed to monitor inventory levels, stock movement, warehouse performance, and product availability, helping businesses optimize inventory management.

Project Type

Inventory Analytics Dashboard

Industry

Manufacturing & Warehousing

Tools

Power BI, DAX, Power Query, Excel

Role

Data Analyst & BI Developer

Project Overview

This Inventory Analytics Dashboard was developed to provide businesses with real-time visibility into inventory levels, stock movement, warehouse performance, and product availability. The dashboard transforms inventory data into actionable insights, helping decision-makers optimize stock levels, reduce shortages and overstocking, improve warehouse efficiency, and support informed inventory planning.

Key Business Metrics

The dashboard tracks key business metrics that provide real-time visibility into inventory performance, warehouse operations and stock availability for more efficient inventory management.

📦 Total Inventory

Displays the total quantity of products currently available across all inventory locations.

📉 Stock Levels

Monitors current stock quantities to ensure products remain adequately stocked.

🔄 Stock Movement

Tracks inventory inflow and outflow to monitor product movement over time.

🏬 Warehouse Performance

Evaluates inventory distribution and operational efficiency across warehouse locations.

⚠️ Low Stock Items

Highlights products approaching reorder levels to prevent stock shortages.

💰 Inventory Value

Calculates the total monetary value of inventory currently in stock.

📋 Reorder Status

Identifies products requiring replenishment based on inventory thresholds.

📊 Product Categories

Shows inventory distribution across different product categories for better planning.

  • ⚠️ Low Stock Alerts
  • 📊 Product Category Analysis
  • 💰 Inventory Value Monitoring
  • 📈 Inventory Trend Analysis
  • 🔍 Interactive Filtering & Drill-down

Business Impact

This dashboard provides inventory managers and business leaders with a centralized view of inventory performance across warehouses, products, and stock levels. By delivering real-time insights into inventory movement, stock availability, warehouse efficiency, and inventory value, the solution supports smarter inventory planning, minimizes stock shortages, reduces excess inventory, and improves operational efficiency.
