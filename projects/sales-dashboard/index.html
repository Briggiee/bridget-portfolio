Sales Performance Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard designed to monitor sales performance, revenue growth, customer trends, and product profitability, enabling business leaders to make data-driven sales decisions.

Project Type

Sales Analytics Dashboard

Industry

Retail & Sales

Tools

Power BI, DAX, Power Query, Excel

Role

Data Analyst & BI Developer

Project Overview

This Sales Performance Dashboard was developed to help business leaders monitor revenue, profit, customer purchasing behavior, and product performance through a single interactive reporting solution. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to identify top-performing products, analyze sales trends, evaluate regional performance, and make informed strategic decisions.

Key Business Metrics

The dashboard tracks key business metrics that help evaluate sales performance, customer behaviour and business growth.

💰 Total Revenue

Total revenue generated from all sales transactions.

💵 Total Profit

Total profit earned after sales expenses.

📦 Orders

Total number of customer orders processed.

👥 Customers

Total unique customers contributing to sales.

📈 Sales Growth

Measures sales growth compared to previous periods.

🛒 Average Order Value

Average amount spent by customers per order.

🏆 Top Products

Identifies the highest-performing products by revenue.

🌍 Regional Sales

Compares sales performance across different regions.

Technologies Used

Power BI DAX Power Query Excel

Dashboard Capabilities

  • 📊 Executive Sales KPI Monitoring
  • 💰 Revenue & Profit Analysis
  • 🛒 Product Performance Tracking
  • 👥 Customer Segmentation
  • 🌍 Regional Sales Analysis
  • 📈 Monthly & Yearly Sales Trends
  • 🎯 Top Performing Products
  • 🔍 Interactive Filtering & Drill-down

Business Impact

This dashboard empowers business executives and sales managers with a centralized view of sales performance across products, customers, and regions. By providing real-time visibility into revenue trends, profit margins, customer behavior, and product performance, the solution supports strategic planning, identifies growth opportunities, and enables faster, data-driven business decisions.
