Wide World Importers Business Intelligence & Advanced Analytics

An end-to-end Business Intelligence and Advanced Analytics solution developed using SQL, Power BI, DAX, Excel and Power Query to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, supply chain operations, supplier activity, profitability, forecasting, financial reporting and warehouse operations.

Project Type

Business Intelligence Dashboard

Industry

Wholesale Distribution / Retail Operations

Tools

SQL, Power BI, DAX, Power Query, Excel

Role

Data Analyst & BI Developer

Project Overview

This Business Intelligence solution was designed to provide decision-makers with a centralized view of Wide World Importers’ commercial and operational performance. The project combines sales, customer activity, supplier performance, supply chain operations, profitability, warehouse metrics, financial reporting and forecasting into a single multi-page analytics solution that supports operational monitoring, executive reporting and strategic decision-making.

Key Business Metrics

The dashboard provides management with a comprehensive view of business performance by tracking revenue, orders, customer activity, profitability, supplier contribution, financial metrics, warehouse operations and forecasting indicators.

💰 Revenue & Sales

Tracks total revenue, order value, quantity sold and sales growth to monitor commercial performance.

👥 Customer Analytics

Measures customer contribution, order behavior, repeat activity and top customer performance across the business.

🚚 Supply Chain Performance

Evaluates procurement trends, supplier contribution and operational movement across the supply chain.

🧾 Supplier Analysis

Monitors supplier concentration, purchase contribution and procurement performance across categories and periods.

📉 Profitability Analysis

Measures total profit, profit margins and cost pressure to understand overall business profitability.

📅 Time Intelligence

Tracks business performance across time to identify growth patterns, seasonality and sales trends.

🔮 Forecasting

Supports planning and forward-looking analysis through demand and revenue forecasting visuals.

🏬 Warehouse & Finance

Monitors stock movement, warehouse distribution, outstanding balances, payment activity and financial reporting indicators.

Technologies Used

SQL Power BI DAX Power Query Excel

Dashboard Capabilities

  • 📊 Executive KPI Monitoring
  • 💰 Sales Performance Analysis
  • 👥 Customer Analytics & Contribution Tracking
  • 🚚 Supply Chain & Supplier Performance Analysis
  • 📉 Profitability & Margin Analysis
  • 📅 Time Intelligence & Trend Monitoring
  • 🔮 Forecasting & Predictive Reporting
  • 🏬 Warehouse, Inventory & Financial Reporting

Business Impact

This dashboard enables decision-makers to monitor sales, customers, suppliers, supply chain performance, profitability, warehouse operations and financial activity through a centralized reporting solution. By consolidating operational and commercial metrics into one interactive dashboard, management can identify revenue drivers, track cost pressure, improve visibility across business functions and make faster data-driven decisions.
