Manufacturing Intelligence Dashboard

Business Intelligence Case Study

Executive Summary

This project demonstrates the design and development of an interactive Manufacturing Intelligence Dashboard using Power BI. The solution consolidates production, inventory, sales, workforce, and supply chain data into a single reporting platform, enabling executives to monitor business performance, identify operational trends, and make informed decisions through interactive visual analytics.

Business Problem

Manufacturing organizations often rely on disconnected reports spread across multiple departments, making it difficult for management to obtain a unified view of operational performance. This lack of centralized reporting can delay decision-making, reduce visibility into key performance indicators, and limit the organization's ability to respond quickly to production, inventory, sales, and supply chain challenges.

Project Objectives

  • Develop an executive dashboard for manufacturing operations.
  • Monitor production performance and operational efficiency.
  • Track sales trends and business profitability.
  • Analyze inventory availability and turnover.
  • Evaluate supplier and supply chain performance.
  • Measure workforce productivity through key operational KPIs.

Dataset Overview

The dashboard was developed using a manufacturing dataset containing production, inventory, sales, supplier, employee, and shipment information. Multiple related tables were modeled in Power BI to provide a comprehensive view of business operations.

Dataset Description
Production Production output, units manufactured and operational performance.
Sales Revenue, orders, customers and profitability analysis.
Inventory Stock levels, warehouse performance and inventory turnover.
Employees Workforce productivity and departmental performance.
Suppliers Supplier performance and procurement activities.
Shipments Logistics, delivery tracking and shipping performance.

Data Preparation

Before building the dashboard, the dataset was cleaned and transformed using Power Query to improve data quality and ensure accurate reporting.

🧹 Data Cleaning

Removed duplicates, corrected inconsistencies and handled missing values.

🔄 Data Transformation

Standardized formats, created calculated columns and transformed fields.

🗓 Calendar Table

Built a dedicated Date table to support time intelligence analysis.

🔗 Data Modeling

Established relationships between tables to create an optimized star schema.

Dashboard Design

The dashboard was designed to provide executives with a clear and interactive overview of manufacturing operations. The layout follows a logical flow, allowing users to quickly understand business performance before exploring detailed operational metrics.

📊 Executive Dashboard

Displays high-level KPIs including production, revenue, inventory, workforce and supply chain performance.

📈 Interactive Visuals

Line charts, bar charts, KPI cards and slicers enable users to analyze trends across different business dimensions.

🎯 User Experience

Clean navigation, consistent colors and responsive layout improve readability and simplify decision-making.

⚡ Performance

Optimized data model and DAX measures ensure efficient report performance and fast filtering.

Key KPIs & DAX Measures

The dashboard incorporates several calculated measures developed using DAX to provide meaningful business insights.

Total Revenue Total Production Total Orders Inventory Value Average Sales Production Efficiency Employee Productivity Supplier Performance

Key Business Insights

Analysis of the manufacturing data revealed several important trends that could help management improve operational efficiency and business performance.

📈 Sales Performance

Revenue trends revealed periods of strong sales performance, highlighting opportunities to maximize profitability during high-demand periods.

📦 Inventory Management

Inventory analysis identified stock movement patterns that can help reduce overstocking and minimize stock shortages.

🏭 Production Efficiency

Production metrics highlighted opportunities to improve operational efficiency and optimize manufacturing output.

🚚 Supply Chain Performance

Supplier and shipment analysis provided visibility into procurement efficiency and delivery performance.

Strategic Recommendations

Based on the insights obtained from the dashboard, the following recommendations can support better business performance.

  • Improve production planning using historical demand trends.
  • Optimize inventory replenishment to reduce excess stock and stockouts.
  • Strengthen supplier performance monitoring through regular KPI reviews.
  • Focus on high-performing products to maximize profitability.
  • Monitor employee productivity continuously to improve operational efficiency.
  • Use dashboard insights during executive meetings for faster data-driven decisions.
